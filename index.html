chicago-coding-resource-hub$

Our mission is to create a library of information and rescources for anyone looking to explore the ever evolving Chicago tech industry. By providing connections to events, education opportunities, and job openings we hope to provide the tools needed to face any challenge.

Education

With how quickly the tech field continues to expand, there are ample learning rescources for both newcomers and veterans. Jump headfirst into our listings to find anything from a day one tutorial to a list of new tricks or forgotten gems for established coders.

Events

Coding involves heavy collaboration and social interaction, what better way to get your feet wet than attending one of the many events around town. Pros in the industry are always giving talks on a wide range of subjects in the field, meetups offer chances to network every day of the week, and workshops pop up all the time.

Job Openings

The end goal: gainful employment in this ever growing and always relevant field. We've partnered with career search engines such as Github Jobs to let you pull up local openings in the same convenient space as our other resources. Utilize our search function to cater the listings towards your particular skillset.
